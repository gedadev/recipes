Potato salad

Best Potatoes for Potato Salad

The secret to a great potato salad is to use the right type of potatoes. They can create a creamy foundation from which other elements like bacon or cheese can shine in the salad bowl.

Ingredients:

  • Potato.
  • Bacon.
  • Scalion.
  • Red onion.
  • Cheese.

Steps:

  1. Place potatoes in a microwave-safe bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and microwave on high for 8 minutes. Set aside.
  2. Make the dressing: in a bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, sour cream, salt, and pepper.
  3. In a large bowl, mix potatoes, sauce, red onion, bacon, scallions, and cheese. Toss gently toss to combine thoroughly.
  4. Transfer the salad to plates to serve.
